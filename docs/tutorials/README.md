## What are Tutorials?

Tutorials are **learning-oriented** lessons that take you by the hand through a series of steps to complete a project. They're designed to help you learn by doing.

### Characteristics of good tutorials:
- ✅ Provide a learning experience
- ✅ Get you started
- ✅ Are safe to explore
- ✅ Are focused on results, not explanations
- ✅ Work for everyone, every time

### What tutorials are NOT:
- ❌ Not how-to guides (those solve specific problems)
- ❌ Not reference material (that's technical description)
- ❌ Not explanations (those provide understanding)
